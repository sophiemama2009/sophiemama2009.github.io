What you need

 

If you are 5 ft or taller, you can try out my cello at the first lesson. If you would like to continue, you can rent a cello from The Violin Shop in Encinitas (https://www.theviolinshopsandiego.com).

 

If you are below 5ft, please rent a cello from The Violin Shop before the first lesson. They can help you find a cello that suites your height.
